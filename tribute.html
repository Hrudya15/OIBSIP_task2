Biography



Chandrasekhara Venkata Raman was born on 7 November, 1888 in Tiruchirappalli. He was as a great Scientist, Physicist, and Nobel laureate, who discovered "Raman Effect".

He had always been a brilliant student and done his Master's in Physics.He has immense curiosity to know about the nature and its secrets. He researched and published many journals including 'Nature', 'Physics Review'etc.He eventually became one of the legendary scientists in the world. In 1924, he was elected as a Fellow of the Royal Society and also he was the first Indian to receive a Nobel Prize in Physics in 1930 .

Awards received by Sir C.V.Raman:

  • Matteucci Medal in 1928
  • Hughes Medal in 1930
  • Nobel Prize in Physics in 1930
  • Bharat Ratna in 1954
  • Lenin Peace Prize in 1957

He took his last breath at the Raman Research Institute on 21st November 1970.In an honor to his works,every year we celebrate National Science Day on 28 February.
